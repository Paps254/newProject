Access to CUK online books and journals.

This is a reminder that up-to-date online books and journals are accessible on CUK RemoteXs platform. The platform facilities access to the resources whether you are on the campus network or off the campus network.

How do I register?

  • Staff can register on RemoteXs by going to link: Create an account
  • The libraby has done bulk registration for students users. Check your email for communication from RenoteXs and follow the instructions given
  • The e-mail contains infornation about how to log in, set your password and oher details.
  • Once your registeration is approved you will receive an e-mail containing information about how to log in, set your password, and other details.
